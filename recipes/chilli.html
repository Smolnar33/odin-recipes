Justin's Hoosier Chili

Description

This is a wonderful chili recipe that I have used to win chili cook-offs in Indiana. It's a hearty, spicy, award-winning chili that's sure to hit the spot on a cold winter's night. Your friends will never guess that the secret ingredient is Campbell's tomato soup. Don't substitute other brands as they don't give the same results.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 teaspoons ground cumin, divided
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 5 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 (15.5 ounce) cans pinto beans, drained (Optional)
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste

Steps

  1. Crumble ground beef into a soup pot over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until evenly browned, 7 to 10 minutes. Drain off all but 1 to 2 tablespoons grease.
  2. Add onion, 1 1/2 teaspoons cumin, and red pepper flakes; cook and stir until onion is tender, about 5 minutes. Pour in chicken broth, condensed soup, and tomatoes; season with chili powder, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Pour in pinto beans, season with remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons cumin and cayenne pepper, and simmer for 30 more minutes.
